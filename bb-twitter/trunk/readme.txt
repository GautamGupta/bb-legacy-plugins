Shows users latest tweet in the profilepage and/or with users posts.

== Description ==

Allows your users to show of their latest tweet in their profilepage and/or with their posts.

== Installation ==

Add `bb-twitter.php` to your `/my-plugins/` directory. And activate from your adminpage. Users controls if/what/where Tweets are shown.


== Configuration ==

Activate on your pluginspage.
